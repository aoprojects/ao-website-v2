Product Manager & UX Designer

My mission:

Offer experiences where users can feel held by those that understand them, so that they feel free to enjoy the task they set out to do.


Strengths:

  • Designing and managing products in favor of greater usability, for customers, and for the company
  • Pragmatic business strategy
  • Maintaining necessary documentation, lists, spreadsheets, and systems

Why Product Management and UX Design?

  • When I started learning to code, I toiled with the idea that being a woman who works in product is not as "feminist" as being a full stack woman coder/hacker.
  • But I kept meeting badass hackers, and wasn't meeting enough people who understood how to acknowledge a user.
  • Meanwhile, I continued to feel frustrated when working with badly designed tools, particularly when I felt that those tools didn't understand me.
  • The highest and best use of my strategic mind and passion seems to be in the creating of experiences.

Specific Goals:

  • Become a known expert in user experience design and product management so that I can increasingly collaborate on projects that really matter in the modern world.
  • Work closely with companies on the sustainability of their ventures and their products.
  • Continue to guide founders in startup and product design best practices, so they can get to their MVPs faster and with less waste than they might experience alone
