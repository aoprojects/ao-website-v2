Founders That Hustle

A membership community of founders helping founders.

Groups meet in person regularly in San Francisco (and sometimes other cities like Seattle or New York) to collaborate on business strategy, recommend vendors, hold one another accountable to sales activity, give early product feedback, and to just hang out.

The format:

Founders in attendance go around the room introducing themselves, each asking for the group's thoughts on a challenge that's actually happening in that founder's startup.

The group votes on 3 winning requests, and workshops on those business challenges together in the moment.

Founders leave with a new plan of attack, and/or having contributed meaningfully to another person's business.

Currently, we are in the MVP (minimum viable product) stage and are managing membership manually with spreadsheets.

Help us grow - join the group and spread the word!
