The Project:

    Standard website redesign:

  • Stay in a CMS-based website so that the client can manage the project after delivery
  • Collaborate on new wording of copy for the website, speaking in a more friendly and direct voice with less jargon
  • Redesign of the customer journey / user flow - allow the user to easily complete the primary business goals of the client (view jobs and request talent) , and to be able to find additional resources (eg. List of happy clients, careers within client’s company, sign up for mailing list)

    • Also learned needed during initial meetings with client:

  • Business owner already owned the .com, rather than just the .tech.
  • Task: move the site over to the .com
  • Client needed a unified online presence, which meant social media banners and a finalized logo in addition to a new website
  • Task: Create social media banner and deliver a reusable vector graphic, refine logo design for site and social media
  • Client wanted to show up on Google Maps as a local provider
  • Task: set up Google My Business account
  • The previous site was mostly static text
  • Task: create custom icons for use in website
  • Since the previous site was uniform in color, add coloring
  • Tasks: add pops of color throughout new site design
  • Client had a desire to better communicate with interested viewers
  • Create an account with a top mailing list manager, and integrate it into the site
  • Recommended next steps for the client to take in order to grow their brand
  • Train client on how to update their site within their CMS
