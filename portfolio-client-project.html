Client Web Redesign Project

A story of discovery of the business' needs.

Project description:

  • Create a more modern and informative website with increased functionality, ensuring that the features users have come to expect from a technical recruiting agency are present
  • Unify the brand across its tech stack - website and email domains, social media graphic designs, brand coloring, email signatures, mailing list, etc.
  • Confirm that the brand is mobile friendly

Time alotted:

  • 2 weeks for site design and site implementation
  • 1 week for domain transfer, social media uploading, and client training

My roles:

  • Business development
  • Project management
  • Product designer and developer

Project Specifics:


    Standard website redesign tasks:

  1. Stay in a CMS-based website so that the client can manage the project after delivery
  2. Redesign of the customer journey / user flow - allow the user to easily complete the primary business goals of the client (view jobs and request talent) , and to be able to find additional resources (eg. List of happy clients, careers within client’s company, sign up for mailing list)
  3. Recommend next steps for the client to take in order to grow their brand, delivered in a well-formatted PDF for ease of use when the client is ready.

    4. Also learned needed during initial meetings with client:

  4. The business already owned the .com domain, rather than just the .tech domain, which their old website was on.
    5. Resulting task:
    Launch the new site on the .com domain
  5. Client needed a unified online presence, and did not have modern social media banners or a finalized logo
    6. Resulting task:
    Create a social media banner and deliver a reusable vector graphic. Refine their new logo design for the website and social media.
  6. Client wanted to show up on Google Maps as a local provider
    7. Resulting task:
    Set up Google My Business account and set it up with their brand assets
  7. The previous website design was made up of mostly text, which did not sound like their human tone of voice.
    8. Resulting tasks:
    Create custom icons for use in website, and work with the team to modify the tone of voice of all web copy
  8. The previous site was designed with just a small amount of one color other than black and white
    9. Resulting tasks:
    Add color pops and a background video for the home page
  9. Client had a desire to better communicate with interested viewers
    10. Resulting task:
    Create a MailChimp account on their behalf, integrate the sign-up form into the website, and set up their MailChimp profile so that it is ready to use.

The result:

A mobile responsive website with a modern design, built in an easy-to-use CMS (content management system), where the client can:

  1. Achieve initial goals they requested

    2. AND

  2. Gather leads through an online form
  3. Accept job applications through their careers page
  4. Collect email addresses through their mailing list
  5. Showcase their company story from the perspective of the founder
  6. Align their brand across their website and their social media; via their new brand colors, new LinkedIn banner, adjustments to their new logo, and updated website copy.
  7. Be found by prospects who primarily search for services by Google Maps

Recommended next steps for the client:

  • Design and branding:
    Create a custom Founder Video for the home page, in which employees speak to the vision of the company, their vision for their clients, and how they are working to achieve it.
  • Marketing and branding:
    Continue to grow the company's mailing list and social media presence in order to draw viewers to the site.

Testimonial from client:

  • "A stellar white-glove approach, a fresh vision for design and reliable communication during the entire process."
