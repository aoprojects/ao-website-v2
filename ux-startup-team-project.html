Startup Venture Mobile App Design

Takeaway from this project:
A product of teamwork and leadership

Project description:

  • Design, and manage the development of, a mobile app product for a new startup venture (and co-found the company).
  • This product will be the core offering of the company.
  • iOS and Android mobile apps will be developed in parallel, and the product will contain several innovative feature sets that are protected by an NDA.
  • To honor that NDA, I have chosen to focus on my work responsibilities themselves, and the role I've played in the progress of the startup so far.

My roles:

  • Product Designer
  • Co-Founder

Time alotted:

  • Ongoing
  • Ideation began in late 2016

Team size:

  • ~ 15 people internally (including 3 co-founders, with the whole team working on equity)
  • our offshore development team

My responsibilities:

  • Assessment of initial idea product-market fit
  • Recommend and assist in set up of internal operational software
  • Competitor analysis
  • Expansion of product core features
  • Definition of features built into the minimum viable product (MVP)
  • Develop product versioning structure/nomenclature
  • Work with team to confirm brand style is in line with product roadmap and company horizons
  • Maintain product roadmap and list of upcoming features, maintaining alignment with business horizons
  • Low fidelity wireframes of MVP
  • Periodically research industry standards and new design concepts, with the goal of choosing the most usable design per feature
  • Collaboration with team to develop high fidelity click-through design with user flow notes incorporated into it [estimated # o slides]
  • User Research within the internal team
  • Gather external and less-biased feedback, per feature idea, while keeping the product vision under NDA
  • Create an internal workflow that gathers input from team on items like potential edge cases, monetization ideas, future feature ideas, design ideas
  • Work with marketing to confirm that all in-app messaging is in alignment with external messaging
  • Provide brand materials for social media accounts
  • Document our Design Rules and share them with the company - these work in sync with the core mission and vision of the company
  • Maintain list of factors that will contribute to future feature decisions, and how the will be incorporated into a living product roadmap
  • Collaborate with strategy, finance, and marketing to design a monetization strategy
  • Participate in fundraising pitch sessions via giving product demos
  • Project management of initial product development with offshore team
  • Regular testing of product versions
  • Periodic gathering and sorting of team input

Lessons learned:

  • There are almost always more edge cases to design.
  • Feedback is almost always useful, especially neutrual feedback
  • Be in the best possible communication with other departments
  • Track tasks - it helps the team pick up pace to see how many there actually are
  • Design directly in Adobe XD (rather than in Photoshop) to build prototypes - it has gotten much more robust lately and is easier to edit along the way
  • Offshore teams can get the job done, but it is best to have a systms architech onshore who is up to date with modern frameworks
  • Create suscinct, very very detailed documentation, but don't let the early lack of it keep things from moving forward
  • Try to laugh more

Next steps:

  • Continue to build out the minimum viable product with the offshore team, while gathering ongoing product feedback and assisting other team members with implementing our launch strategy.
